About

Two people who got tired of watching good crews get bad information.

Clean Money Corporation is an S-corporation built around one idea: construction software should be honest, usable in the field, and priced like a tool — not a subscription maze.

The origin story is short.

We'd both spent years watching construction companies pay thousands per month for software their foremen refused to open. Workers were on paper. Hours were late. MROs got lost. The "enterprise solution" was a PowerPoint, not a product.

So we built what we wished existed. A phone-first app that a crew can install at lunch and use the rest of the day. No training course. No "consultant onboarding engagement". No surprise invoices.

That product is CHERP. Clean Money Corporation sells it.

How we work.

Four rules we don't break.

1

Honest pricing

One rate, one invoice. If we add a feature, it's in the existing tier. No "call for pricing" games.

2

Field-first testing

Every feature ships through a real crew before it ships to customers. If it doesn't survive a Monday, it doesn't survive release.

3

Data belongs to you

Full export to CSV, PDF, or Google Sheets, any time. If you cancel, you walk away with everything.

4

Fast, not flashy

Phones are small. Signals are weak. We optimize for the 2G-in-a-basement day, not the gigabit-in-an-office day.
