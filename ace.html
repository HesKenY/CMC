Why ACE beats what you're using now.

Most construction software is built by people who've never used a chop saw. ACE is different in three specific, measurable ways.

1. Offline-first, not offline-capable

Your current app "supports offline" — but you've probably still lost data from a basement pour. ACE writes to the phone first, then syncs, every time. There's no "network required for this action" popup.

2. Install in 10 minutes, not 10 days

No vendor onboarding call. No 40-page PDF. Foremen hand out a link, workers install, everyone is running by afternoon coffee. We've done it in under an hour with 20-person crews.

3. Honest flat pricing

One rate per active crew. No per-seat surprise. No "oh you need the accounting module, that's extra." See the pricing section below — it's the whole invoice.
